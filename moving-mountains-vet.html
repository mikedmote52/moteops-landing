Why In-Home Rehab
Changes Everything

Most rehab clinics see 8–10 patients a day. Dr. Mote left hospital practice because she wanted more. You get her full attention, in your home, for as long as it takes.

Familiar Environment

Pets heal faster when they're calm. Their own home — their smells, their bed — reduces stress hormones that slow recovery.

🚫

No Painful Car Rides

Loading a post-op or arthritic pet into a car twice a week is hard on them — and on you. We come to you, every time.

Unhurried, Thorough Care

📋

Custom Rehab Plan

Every patient gets a plan built around their specific surgery, condition, age, and lifestyle — not a protocol designed for the average dog.

🐈

Cats Welcome

7 Days a Week

What We Treat

Rehabilitation Services

Evidence-based modalities, delivered in your living room.

🏋️

Therapeutic Exercise

Targeted movement programs that rebuild strength, coordination, and confidence after injury or surgery.

🤲

Manual Therapy & Massage

Hands-on joint mobilization and soft tissue work to reduce pain, improve circulation, and restore range of motion.

💡

Laser Therapy (Photobiomodulation)

Low-level laser stimulates cellular repair, reduces inflammation, and accelerates healing in injured tissue.

🔄

Range of Motion & Stretching

Systematic joint mobilization to prevent contracture, reduce stiffness, and maintain functional movement patterns.

🩺

Pain Management

Multimodal approach combining physical techniques and guidance on medical management in coordination with your vet.

🔧

Post-Surgical Recovery

Structured protocols for TPLO, TTA, fracture repair, spinal surgery — week-by-week milestones and clear expectations.

🌿

Geriatric & Senior Care

Helping aging pets stay mobile, comfortable, and engaged in life — managing arthritis, muscle atrophy, and cognitive changes.

⚖️

Weight Management

Customized conditioning programs for overweight pets — especially those who can't tolerate high-impact exercise.

Built Around Charlie.
