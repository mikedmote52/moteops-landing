The work

Six AI tools, running in production.

Two I built for myself. Four I built for clients. Screenshots below — I walk through any of them live on the call.

Built for myself
Personal · Live · YouTube · Podcasts · Books
LearnEngine

YouTube. Podcasts. Books. Paste a video link, drop in a podcast URL, or photograph a book chapter — LearnEngine reads it cover-to-cover and builds a custom quiz of any size, fifteen to a hundred questions. Miss something? It generates flashcards on the spots you got wrong, scheduled by an FSRS engine that resurfaces each card the exact day you're about to forget it. Used to be "I watched the video, hope it stuck." Now it's "I watched the video, the app made sure it stuck." Walk-through on the call.

Personal · Live · Paper
Squeeze Prophet

An autonomous trading bot I built for myself. Scans the market across each session — pre-market, midday, power hour, close — places paper trades on signal, reports throughout the day. The dashboard surfaces cohort breakdowns, weight drift, regime detection — the system thinking out loud. Walk-through on the call.

Built for clients
Client · Live
CC's Learning Center

Built my mom's family-owned childcare site in Brea plus a 24/7 AI parent chatbot. Fifteen years of service, now with intake and after-hours questions handled automatically. The bot books tours, answers parents at any hour, and pipes leads into an owner dashboard while a competitive-intel sweep tracks the local chains.
Client · Live
CoreVita Collective

Built the full clinical SaaS for a solo nurse practitioner running a nurse-led wellness practice in Newport Beach, capped at fifteen members on purpose. AI drafts each session brief from her notes and surfaces trends across weeks of client data, but nothing reaches a member until she's reviewed and approved it. Phase tracking, embedded Stripe billing, compliance-aware: one practitioner operating like a small clinic.
Client · Live
Moving Mountains Vet

Built a two-sided system for Dr. Christina Mote, DVM in Denver: a mobile rehab booking front-end for pet families plus a relief-staffing marketplace pairing credentialed DVMs with Colorado hospitals during the state's vet shortage. Rules-based matching, Stripe placements, and credential-renewal tracking keep both sides moving without a coordinator in the middle.
Client · Spreadsheet
Danny's E&S Appetite Guide

Built carrier intelligence covering 113 commercial property markets and 42 carriers for Danny, a wholesale insurance broker working California E&S. Refreshed monthly, queried daily from his iPad in the car between meetings. Color signals show which carriers will write each property class before he picks up the phone, because the right tool here was a spreadsheet, not a Next.js dashboard.
