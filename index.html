Teaching

Workflows you'll learn.

Each one runs in my own life today. I'll wire one up for your business, then walk you through how to maintain it yourself.

Daily inbox triage

Each morning an agent reads your unread mail, drafts safe replies, opens action URLs in your browser ready for one tap, and texts you a one-line summary.

Best fit: Executives, professionals, anyone losing 90 minutes a day to email.
System health briefings

A daily 8 AM brief reads system messages and alerts, classifies issues, queues auto-fixes for known problems, and tells you only what needs your hands.

Best fit: Founders running multiple tools, small ops teams.
Multi-platform listing automation

Your listing posts to every platform automatically. Inbound messages get classified, prospects auto-tracked, silent threads archive themselves and reactivate if the prospect comes back.

Best fit: Rental property managers, marketplace sellers, anyone running listings across more than one site.
Daily intelligence scout

Each morning an agent reads the day's AI, tech, and industry news and filters down to items that actually impact your specific projects, with named integration recommendations.

Best fit: Builders, founders, anyone trying to stay current without drowning.
Pre-market trading brief

A scan of your portfolio plus market state, surfaced as a clean morning text with positions approaching stops, profit targets, and continuation watchlist. The bot watches; you decide.

Best fit: Active investors, traders, anyone managing a real-money book.
Adaptive learning loop

Turns any video, podcast, or book into a quiz tailored to you, then resurfaces the concepts you missed weeks later, right when you're about to forget them.

Best fit: Knowledge workers, students, anyone who reads or watches a lot but retains too little.
