Frequently Asked Questions

What if my leads are really old? ▼
The older the better, actually. Leads from 6-24 months ago often had a genuine need that wasn't fulfilled. The AI references their original inquiry, which creates instant recognition. We've seen our best response rates from leads that went cold 12+ months ago.
Will this feel spammy to my leads? ▼
No. Each message is personalized and conversational. We reference their specific project type and timeframe. Response rates of 8-15% prove people welcome the outreach. They remember your company, and the AI's natural tone makes it feel like a real person reaching out, not a bot.
What CRM do I need? ▼
Any CRM that can export a CSV works. We integrate directly with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, and GoHighLevel. For others, a simple spreadsheet export is all we need. If you're not sure, reach out and we'll figure it out together.
How fast do I see results? ▼
Most campaigns generate their first hot lead within 48 hours. Full campaign results in 7 days. You'll have a complete performance report with ROI breakdown, including which leads engaged, who's hot vs. warm, and exactly what they said. No mystery.
