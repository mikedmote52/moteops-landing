Form factor

Spreadsheet, not web app.

Danny's workflow is structured cells refreshed monthly, queried daily on his iPad in the car between meetings. The right tool for this job was a spreadsheet, not a Next.js dashboard. Honest answer beats clever answer.

What's tracked

Carriers, classes, dates, exclusions.

Each row encodes appetite, A.M. Best rating, broker contact, last-updated date, and the language exclusions that move a Y to a N. Danny knows before he calls who's actually going to quote.
