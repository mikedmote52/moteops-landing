Scaling Roadmap

Five-phase plan from bootstrap to $20K+/month

Phase 1 — Months 1–3
Bootstrap with Consulting
Build fractional AI consultant practice. Land 2–3 retainer clients. Generate case studies and cash to fund everything else.
Target: $4K–9K/mo
Phase 2 — Months 3–6
Add Product Plays
Leverage consulting clients into product customers. Implement missed-call and review-response systems. Bundle consulting + product for hybrid revenue.
Target: $7.5K–12K/mo
Phase 3 — Months 6–12
Vertical Expansion
Expand beyond home services into dental, real estate, fitness, legal. Reuse proven automations across verticals.
Target: $15K–25K/mo
Phase 4 — Months 12–18
Productize as Templates
Package experience into n8n template bundles. Create industry-specific automation packs. Passive income layer begins compounding.
Target: $20K–40K/mo
Phase 5 — Months 18–24
Scale or Acquire
Decision point: stay solo at high margin or hire for 10x growth. Use cash flow to acquire distressed products or double down on winners.
Target: $20K–85K/mo
